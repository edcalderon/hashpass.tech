# Speaker Onboarding Guide

Complete step-by-step guide for speakers to manage meetings and schedules.

## Step 1: Sign In

Access your speaker dashboard.

1. Open the HashPass app.
2. Enter your registered email address.
3. Check your email for the one-time login code.
4. Enter the code to access your speaker account.

## Step 2: View Meeting Requests

You receive notifications when attendees request meetings with you.

- Check the Notifications icon in the app header.
- Review pending meeting requests from attendees.
- See attendee name, preferred time, and message details.
- Review all requests in the notifications panel.

## Step 3: Accept Meeting Requests

When you are ready to accept a request:

1. Open the meeting request notification.
2. Review the attendee message and preferred time.
3. Select **Accept Request**.
4. Pick an available time slot from your schedule.
5. Confirm the meeting details.
6. The attendee is notified automatically.

## Step 4: Manage Your Schedule

Manage your availability and meetings in the schedule view.

- View all confirmed meetings.
- Set available meeting slots.
- Update meeting details if needed.
- Cancel meetings if your schedule changes.
- Review upcoming meetings in chronological order.

## Step 5: Decline Requests

If you cannot accept a meeting request:

1. Open the request.
2. Select **Decline**.
3. Add a message if needed.
4. The attendee is notified respectfully.

> Tip: A brief explanation is helpful, but optional.

## Troubleshooting

If the web app is not loading correctly:

- Clear your browser cache and reload the page.
- Hard refresh with `Ctrl+Shift+R` on Windows/Linux or `Cmd+Shift+R` on macOS.
